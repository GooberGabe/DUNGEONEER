
If you want to use these cards with the fake portal effect,
you can buy the Source version which includes the project files
for Unreal Engine, Godot and Unity.

Otherwise, you can research a method called Stencil Buffer.
Here's an example tutorial for Unity:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y-SEiDTbszk

If you don't want to mess with shaders, you can just have
a flat unlit color for the background of your scene,
and set an unlit material with the same color to the exterior 
of the cards.
